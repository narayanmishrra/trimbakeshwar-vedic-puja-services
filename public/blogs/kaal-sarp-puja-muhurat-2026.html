Understanding Kaal Sarp Shanti Puja

Kaal Sarp Yoga occurs when all seven main astrological planets in a birth chart fall between the shadow nodes, Rahu and Ketu. Performing Kaal Sarp Dosh Nivaran Puja on auspicious days such as Panchami, Amavasya, Mondays, or specific Nakshatras brings rapid relief from stagnation, career delays, and health issues.

Nag Panchami (17 August 2026): This is the supreme day of the year to seek forgiveness from Snake Deities. Initiating Kaal Sarp Shanti on this day yields immediate spiritual benefits.

Amavasya (New Moon): Dark moon nights are highly favorable for performing remedies for shadow planets Rahu and Ketu.

Mondays: The sacred day dedicated to Lord Shiva, the master of time and planetary systems, is highly recommended for Kalsarpa remedies.
